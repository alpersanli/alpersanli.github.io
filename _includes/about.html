RTL-SDR is the entry ticket into the invisible world of radio waves. A $30 USB dongle can turn any laptop into a spectrum analyzer, a weather satellite receiver, or even a gateway to amateur radio astronomy.

Popular experiments include decoding NOAA weather images, tracking airplanes via ADS-B at 1090 MHz, or listening to ship AIS broadcasts. With tools like SatDump and dump1090, the entire RF spectrum becomes a playground.

Tips for better results:

  • Use an LNA close to the antenna to reduce noise figure.
  • Account for Doppler shift when tracking satellites in low Earth orbit.
  • Archive IQ data‚Äîyou can re-process it with improved algorithms later.
