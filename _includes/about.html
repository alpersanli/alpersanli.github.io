Modulation Transfer Function (MTF) and Point Spread Function (PSF) are two pillars of modern optics. While PSF describes how a single point of light spreads through an optical system, MTF quantifies how different spatial frequencies are transmitted.

In satellite payloads, these metrics are not just theoretical‚Äîthey directly define ground resolution and image sharpness. Engineers often balance between diffraction limits, alignment tolerances, and structural stability to achieve the desired performance.
